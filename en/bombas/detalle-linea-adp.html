ADP Line

Diaphragm Dosing Pump for General Use

Dosing / General Purpose

General Description

The ADP Series pumps are widely used in laboratory and industrial dosing applications due to their ability to deliver exact volumes.

Their robust construction in AISI 316 and Viton guarantees excellent chemical resistance. Flow rates are highly adjustable, starting from 0.2 lts/h, thanks to the use of variable frequency drives (VFD) acting on the motor.
