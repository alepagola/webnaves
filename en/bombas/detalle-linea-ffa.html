FFA Line

Quick-Clean Sanitary Pump

Food Grade / Standard

General Description

The FFA Line is designed specifically for the food and beverage industry. Manufactured in AISI 304 or AISI 316 with a mirror-polished finish, it guarantees hygiene and prevents bacterial buildup.

Its quick-clean design facilitates daily cleaning processes (CIP/COP). The connections can be configured with Danish threaded unions (DIN) or Clamp-type quick couplings, depending on your needs.

Why choose the FFA Line?
Total Hygiene: Sanitary polish and the absence of dead spaces to prevent contamination.
Maintenance: Quick-clean disassembly system requiring no special tools.
Gentle Handling: Smooth pumping action that doesn't damage the product's structure (e.g., fruit yogurts).
