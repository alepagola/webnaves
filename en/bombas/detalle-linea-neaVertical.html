Vertical NEA Line

Vertical Oil Pump for API Separators and Sumps

Immersion / Semi-Submersible

General Description

The Vertical NEA Line is specially designed to operate in API/TK separators and drain sumps. Its semi-submersible configuration allows for the efficient pumping of fluids with suspended solids from variable depths.

It features robust drive heads that ensure smooth, vibration-free operation. Additionally, its mounting plates (standard flanges) facilitate direct installation on existing tank manways.

Technical Specifications

Max Flow Rate Up to 70 m³/h
Max Pressure Up to 12 bar
Max Temperature 150° C
Max Viscosity Up to 1,000,000 cP
Rotor Material 316 Stainless Steel / Hard Chrome
Available Stators Nitrile, EPDM, Viton (Hydrocarbon resistant)
Connection Standard Flanges 10” / 14” / 20”

  • API Separators (Water/Oil separators).
  • Drain Sumps.
  • Slop Tanks.
  • Drainage Recovery.

The pumps are adapted to the required sump depths. Baseplates are available in standard sizes of 10”, 14”, and 20”. Please refer to the data sheet for detailed drawings.

Why choose the Vertical NEA?
Easy Installation: "Drop-in" design for existing tanks.
Reliability: Reinforced drive head to prevent vibrations on the long shaft.
Versatility: Handles dirty crude, sludge, and hydrocarbon-contaminated water.
