J Line

Industrial Pump with Open Hopper and Auger Feed Screw

General Description

The J Line is designed with an open throat hopper and an auger feed screw. This configuration is indispensable for pumping non-flowable products and those with extreme viscosities that cannot be gravity-fed.

The auger forces the product into the stator, ensuring complete filling of the cavities and preventing cavitation when handling thick fluids like pastes or dewatered sludge.
