Comprehensive Services

Applied engineering to optimize your equipment's performance.

More than a supplier, your technical partner

We don't just manufacture pumps; we offer engineering solutions to overhaul, adapt, and upgrade your existing pumping equipment, regardless of the brand.

Retrofitting & Standardization

Technical independence and guaranteed savings.

Do you own pumps from other brands (Netzsch, Moyno, Seepex) where spare parts are expensive or take months to arrive?

We provide the necessary adaptation engineering to modify your existing equipment, allowing them to use our standard spare parts.

  • Drastic reduction in maintenance costs.
  • Spare parts delivery in 48/72 hours.
  • No modifications needed to your original piping or installation.

Custom Multi-brand Spare Parts

We manufacture compatible parts for any brand of progressing cavity pump on the market. We utilize 3D scanning technology and reverse engineering to reproduce rotors, stators, shafts, and connecting rods with millimeter precision.

We can upgrade the original materials (e.g., switching from carbon steel to stainless steel, or changing the elastomer compound) to extend the lifespan of your equipment.

Comprehensive Overhaul

We have a specialized department for repairing both our own equipment and third-party pumps. The service includes:

  • Teardown and detailed technical diagnosis.
  • Shot blasting and epoxy painting.
  • Replacement of seals, bearings, and O-rings.
  • Hydraulic bench testing prior to delivery.

Commissioning & Start-up

We offer on-site technical assistance for the start-up of new equipment. We train your maintenance and operations personnel on proper use, torquing, and preventive care, ensuring flawless operation from day one.

Engineering & Documentation

We provide the most complete technical documentation in the market to integrate our equipment into your projects:

  • 3D and 2D CAD Drawings.
  • Data Books (Welding qualifications, procedures).
  • Test protocols and material certificates.

Logistics & Delivery

We understand the urgency of your operations. We have our own fleet to handle direct deliveries to:

  • Buenos Aires Metro Area (C.A.B.A. & G.B.A.).
  • Freight forwarders (for nationwide and regional shipping).

We ensure that all equipment arrives at its destination on time and in perfect condition, using proper protective packaging.
