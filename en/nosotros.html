About Us

Regional Leaders in Pump Manufacturing

GECE PUMPS® is a registered trademark and division of Naves del Sur S.A., dedicated to the manufacturing and marketing of a complete line of progressing cavity pumps for all types of industries.

In our 1,800 m² manufacturing plant, we feature state-of-the-art CNC machinery, facilities specifically designed for our manufacturing processes, and highly trained personnel. We manufacture pumps for all industries: oil and gas, chemical, wastewater, food and beverage, cement, paints, drilling muds, and dosing.

If you have a pumping challenge or need spare parts, do not hesitate to contact us.
